Nakitai Watashi wa Neko wo Kaburu (A Whisker Away Movie)

The story revolves around Miyo Sasaki, a high school girl who has a crush on her classmate Kento Hinode. However, her attempts to get his attention are often clumsy and unsuccessful. One day, she encounters a mysterious mask seller named "The Mask Seller," who gives her a magical cat mask. This mask allows her to transform into a cat, and as a cat, she gets closer to Kento and observes his life. She enjoys the freedom and escape from her troubles, but she soon realizes that there are consequences to this magical ability.
